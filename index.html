WELCOME TO Pragya Higher Secondary School Jadonpur Milak Rampur

Pragya Higher Secondary School was established in 1994 by our Co-founder Mr.Harpal Singh. Our focus is over all development of student which means physical mental and spiritual development .Immigration of talent is one of the major concern of our country , so keeping this in mind we have tried to develop an education system which fills student with positive thinking nationalism and patriotism. We provides many facilities for the enhancement of our students .

Classrooms

Our classrooms are spacious, airy, well lit, ventilate with display & writing boards, custom made furniture.We Maintain the classroom with in a proper span of time. we think that if there'll be clean classrooms,productivity of students will increase.

Yoga and Meditation

In terms of energy Yoga is like a universe in itself. Some of the important Yoga benefits include anti-ageing, balance and flexibility of body, increase in knowledge and wealth, improvement in mental health and development of personal and social values.

Parents Orientation Program

Time to time Pragya Higher Secondary School organizes Parent’s Orientation Programme where teachers & Principal discuss the various facts of child’s personality with parents. Remedies to their problems are found out to nurture the progress of a child.

Sport Activity

The benefits of physical activity for the young are manifold. Sports give an opportunity to develop their physical skills & to remain healthy and active, while experiencing the pleasure of achievement. This also teaches them the qualities of self-control & leadership .

Cultural Activity

A substantial number of hours in the time table and ample opportunity after are dedicated to encouraging children to explore a wide range of creative pursuits. Time spent on music, drama, dance and painting, as well as on scientific interests is time well spent.

Security

Safety and learning go hand-in-hand. Students who feel that they are not adequately supported and safe at school, both physically and psychologically, cannot learn to their fullest potential.we also have CCTV survillance in our campus as well as outside the campus.

Golden Memories

We believe in a transparent and open admission process where deserving students get an opportunity irrespective of their backgrounds.
